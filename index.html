About Our Academy

Founded in 1995, Divine Grace Academy has been at the forefront of providing quality education that balances academic achievement with personal growth.

Our campus spans over 20 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including science laboratories, art studios, athletic fields, and a modern library.

We believe in educating the whole child - mind, body, and spirit - to prepare them for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.

Our Journey

Over 25 years of educational excellence and community impact

1995

Foundation

Divine Grace Academy was founded by Dr. and Mrs. Robert Johnson with just 45 students in grades K-5, operating from a small church building.

2002

New Campus

Moved to our current 25-acre campus with state-of-the-art facilities, expanding to include middle school grades.

2008

High School Launch

Added high school program and graduated our first senior class. Achieved accreditation from the National Association of Independent Schools.

2015

20th Anniversary

Celebrated 20 years of excellence with over 1,200 alumni. Opened the Innovation & Technology Center.

2020

Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital learning platform and launched global virtual exchange programs.

2023

Today & Beyond

Serving 850 students with 98% college acceptance rate. Continuing our commitment to innovative Christian education.

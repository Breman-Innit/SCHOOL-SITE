About Our Academy

Founded in 1995, Divine Grace Academy has been at the forefront of providing quality education that balances academic achievement with personal growth.

Our campus spans over 20 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including science laboratories, art studios, athletic fields, and a modern library.

We believe in educating the whole child - mind, body, and spirit - to prepare them for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.

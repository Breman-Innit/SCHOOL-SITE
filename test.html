Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about touring our campus

What should I expect during a campus tour?

During your campus tour, you'll have the opportunity to see our facilities, observe classrooms in session (when available), meet with faculty and staff, and get a feel for our school community. Tours typically last 60-90 minutes and include time for questions.

Can my child accompany me on the tour?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to join the tour. It's a great opportunity for them to imagine themselves as part of our community. For younger children, we recommend bringing them along to see if they feel comfortable in our environment.

How far in advance should I schedule a tour?

We recommend scheduling your tour at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak admission seasons (September-November and January-March). This ensures we can accommodate your preferred date and time.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all local health guidelines to ensure the safety of our visitors and community. Currently, we offer both in-person and virtual tour options. For in-person tours, we may have specific protocols in place which will be communicated to you before your visit.

Is there parking available on campus?

Yes, we have designated visitor parking available. When you arrive on campus, please check in at the main office where our staff will direct you to available parking and greet you for your tour.
